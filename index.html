Frequently Asked

What is the cost of a puppy? expand_more
Puppies start at $2,000, and $3,000 for the merle puppies. We require a $500 non-refundable deposit to secure your pick of the litter.
Where are you located? expand_more
We are based in Columbus, Indiana. Local pickup is welcome, and we love for our families to see where their puppy was raised.
Do you ship or offer delivery? expand_more
Yes. We can safely arrange delivery anywhere within the United States using professional flight nannies or climate-controlled ground transport.
What kind of homes are you looking for? expand_more
We look for responsible, committed owners who understand the needs of large, powerful breeds. Ideal homes have adequate space and are dedicated to lifelong training and socialization.
Are the dogs good with kids and other animals? expand_more
Our dogs are raised around people and everyday environments. XL Bullies are known as "gentle giants," and our breeding focus on temperament ensures they are stable family companions.
What is included with a puppy? expand_more
Puppies go home healthy, well-socialized, and raised in a family environment. They come with a health guarantee, current vaccinations, deworming, and lifetime breeder support.
Any other questions or ready to move forward? expand_more
Feel free to fill out our 60-second application form and we'll get back to you in less than 24 hours guaranteed!
